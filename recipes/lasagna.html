Lasagna

Description

This is a piece of Lasagna. It's layers consist of noodle dough
and it is filled with tomato sauce, beef and/or some vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Noodle Plates
  • Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Fry the vegetables.
  2. Add the tomato sauce.
  3. Put a layer of sauce on the bottom of an oiled pan.
  4. Cover the sauce with some noodle plates.
  5. Repeat until all ingredients are used.
  6. Use noodle plates for the cover of the lasagna and cover it with cheese.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes at 200 degrees Â°C.
